Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

Welcome to LFI Writing Guide Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Our LFI Writing Guide Pro tool is designed to run entirely on your local device (client-side). We do not collect, store, or have access to any of the data you input into the tool's forms.

  • Personal Data: We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our main tool. If you choose to contact us via email, we will collect your email address and any information you provide in your message to respond to your inquiry.
  • Usage Data: Like most websites, our server may automatically log non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit. This information is used for statistical analysis to improve our website and is not linked to any personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain our website and services.
  • To respond to your questions, comments, and requests when you contact us directly.
  • To monitor and analyze usage to improve the user experience.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Cookies

This website may display advertisements from third-party networks, including Google AdSense. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

The cookies of other third-party vendors or ad networks may also be used to serve ads on this site. We do not have control over these cookies. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since we do not collect personal data through our tool, the primary risk is standard to all internet browsing. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect our website, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
